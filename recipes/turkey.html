Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

Description

This is a recipe for a Turkey Sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of any kind of bread
  • Sliced deli Turkey
  • Lettuce(optional)
  • 2 pieces of any kind of cheese(optional)
  • Mayonnaise(optional)

Steps

  1. Take 2 pieces of bread
  2. Place the turkey slices on the slices of bread
  3. Place the cheese on top of the turkey
  4. place the lettuce on top of the cheese and turkey
  5. Spread the Mayonnaise on top and place the 2 sides together
  6. Enjoy!
