Ramen Noodle Soup

Description

This is a recipe for Ramen Noodle Soup.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 (3.5 oz) package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Steps

  1. Gather the Ingredients
  2. Combine broth and noodles in a medium saucepan; cover and bring to a boil over high heat, stirring to break up noodles.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chili oil, and ginger. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in sesame oil.
  5. Garnish with green onions and serve.
