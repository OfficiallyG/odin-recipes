Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

Description

This is a recipe for a Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of any kind of bread
  • 1 jar of any kind of Jelly

Steps

  1. Take 2 pieces of bread.
  2. Spread the peanut butter on one side with a knife.
  3. Spread the Jelly on the other side with a knife.
  4. Enjoy!
